# LinkedIn Post — Uppsala Azure User Group Launch

---

For a while now, cities like Stockholm and Västerås have had their own Azure User Groups. Amazing communities where people meet, share knowledge, and geek out about all things Microsoft cloud.

But Uppsala? Nothing. Until now.

We're starting **Uppsala Azure User Group (UAUG)** and we'd love for you to be part of it.

Our ambition is simple: find like-minded people who enjoy working within the Microsoft ecosystem, whether you're deep into Azure, just getting started, or simply curious about where the cloud is heading. Developers, IT pros, architects, students, everyone is welcome.

**Our kickoff event is May 7, 2026**, and we have two fantastic sessions lined up.

Food, beverages, and good conversations included. The venue is at Viedoc, Stationsgatan 23 in Uppsala.

Head over to **uppsalaazure.com** to check out the event and sign up.

This is just the beginning. Our hope is to build a community that keeps growing and meeting regularly. That only happens with people who show up and share what they know. So if you have an Azure topic you're passionate about, submit a session for an upcoming event on our website. The best user groups are the ones built by their members.

See you in Uppsala, let's make this happen together!
