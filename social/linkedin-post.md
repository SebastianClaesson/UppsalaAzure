# LinkedIn Post — Uppsala Azure User Group Launch

---

**Exciting news for the tech community in Uppsala!**

We're thrilled to announce the launch of **Uppsala Azure User Group (UAUG)** — a free, community-driven meetup for anyone interested in Microsoft Azure and cloud technologies.

Whether you're a developer, IT pro, architect, student, or just cloud-curious — you're welcome.

**Our first event: May 7, 2026**
- Two sessions by amazing speakers
- Bjorn Sundling (Microsoft MVP) — *Bicep: From Beginner to Pro*
- Sebastian Claesson — *Maester: Azure DevOps Security*
- Food, beverages, and networking included
- Venue: Viedoc, Stationsgatan 23, Uppsala

Spots are limited to 35 — register now at **uppsalaazure.com**

We'd also love to hear from potential speakers! If you have an Azure topic you're passionate about, submit a session on our website.

Let's build something great together. See you in Uppsala!
