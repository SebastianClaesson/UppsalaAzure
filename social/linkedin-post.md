# LinkedIn Post — Uppsala Azure User Group Launch

---

We're starting **Uppsala Azure User Group (UAUG)** and we'd love for you to be part of it.

Our ambition is simple: find like-minded people who enjoy working within the Microsoft ecosystem, whether you're deep into Azure, just getting started, or simply curious about where the cloud is heading. Developers, IT pros, architects, students, everyone is welcome.

**Our kickoff event is May 7, 2026** with two sessions:
- Björn Sundling (Microsoft MVP) on Bicep, from beginner to pro
- Sebastian Claesson on using AI to review and strengthen your Azure foundation

Food, beverages, and good conversations included. The venue is at Viedoc, Stationsgatan 23 in Uppsala.


This is just the beginning. Our hope is to build a community that keeps growing and meeting regularly. If you have an Azure topic you're passionate about, submit a session for an upcoming event on our website.

See you in Uppsala, let's make this happen together!
