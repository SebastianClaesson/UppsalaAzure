# Facebook Post — Uppsala Azure User Group Launch

---

Hey Uppsala! We're starting something new.

**Uppsala Azure User Group (UAUG)** is a free community for anyone curious about Microsoft Azure and cloud tech. No experience required — beginners and experts are equally welcome.

Our very first meetup is on **May 7** and we have two great sessions lined up:

- Bjorn Sundling (Microsoft MVP) — Bicep: From Beginner to Pro
- Sebastian Claesson — Maester: Azure DevOps Security

We'll kick things off at 17:30 with food and drinks, followed by the sessions and networking until 20:00. It's completely free — all you need to do is sign up.

Where: Viedoc, Stationsgatan 23, Uppsala
When: May 7, 2026 — 17:30 to 20:00
Spots: Only 35 available

Register and learn more at uppsalaazure.com

Know someone who'd be interested? Tag them below! And if you want to give a talk at a future event, you can submit a session on our website too.

See you there!
