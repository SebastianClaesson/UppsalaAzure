About Us

Meet the people behind UAUG.

Uppsala Azure User Group

UAUG is a local community for anyone interested in Microsoft Azure — from beginners to seasoned cloud architects. We meet regularly in Uppsala to share knowledge, learn from each other, and stay up to date with the Azure ecosystem. Our events are free and open to everyone.

Code of Conduct

UAUG is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone.

All attendees, speakers, sponsors, and organizers at Uppsala Azure User Group events are required to follow this Code of Conduct. We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and harassment-free environment regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or technical experience level.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful and considerate in your speech and actions
  • Be open to different viewpoints and experiences
  • Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
  • Focus on what is best for the community
  • Show empathy towards other community members

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
  • Offensive verbal comments related to personal characteristics
  • Inappropriate physical contact or unwelcome attention
  • Sustained disruption of talks or events
  • Publishing others' private information without permission
  • Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

Enforcement

Attendees who violate this Code of Conduct may be asked to leave the event immediately and without refund, at the discretion of the organizers. We reserve the right to remove anyone from current and future events who does not comply with these guidelines.

Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any concerns, please contact the organizers directly at the event or email Register@UppsalaAzure.tech. All reports will be handled with discretion.
